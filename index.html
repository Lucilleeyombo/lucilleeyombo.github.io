Portfolio Artifacts

Digital Health Education Program: Bariatric Surgery & GLP-1 Patient Support

Tool: GrowthHub 365 | Focus: Digital Health Education, Patient Engagement, Instructional Design

Situation
Patients undergoing bariatric surgery or using GLP-1 medications need structured education to support long-term lifestyle change, adherence, nutrition, emotional regulation, and sustainable success.
Task
I designed a digital education program that translated complex clinical and behavioral guidance into clear, practical, patient-friendly content.
Action
I created a structured course in GrowthHub 365 organized into five core areas: Bariatric Mindset Reset, Emotional Eating Recovery Toolbox, Protein/Water/Vitamins, Boundaries & Food Pushers, and Happy Off The Scale.
Result
The project produced a complete digital course framework that made complex health education easier to understand and positioned the content for scalable use with adult learners.
Learning
This project strengthened my ability to design user-centered digital health education and showed how clear structure can improve patient engagement.

Strategic Portfolio Management & Governance Framework

Tool: Smartsheet | Focus: Governance, Strategic Alignment, Portfolio Management

Medical Equipment Procurement & Handover Project

Tools: Excel, Word, Canva, WhatsApp, Email | Focus: Healthcare Project Leadership, Logistics, Stakeholder Engagement

Situation
Muyuka District Hospital needed additional medical, laboratory, maternal, and surgical equipment to strengthen patient care and hospital service capacity.
Task
I chaired the project committee and coordinated the medical equipment procurement and handover initiative from planning through delivery and public presentation.
Action
I coordinated stakeholders, supported procurement planning, tracked logistics, developed communication materials, and helped organize the official handover ceremony. The project included international freight logistics totaling 1,600,500 FCFA.
Result
Equipment included patient monitors, standard infant warmers, delivery forceps, binocular microscopes, semi-automatic chemistry analyzers, auto hematology analyzers, mould incubators, operating LED lamps, and surgical support instruments.
Learning
This project strengthened my leadership in multi-stakeholder coordination, cross-border logistics, communication, accountability, and healthcare project execution.

Customer Satisfaction & Program Performance Analysis

Tool: Microsoft Excel | Focus: Data Analysis, Quality Improvement, Performance Reporting

Situation
Healthcare programs needed clearer insight into satisfaction data to identify service gaps, improve patient experience, and support quality improvement decisions.
Task
I analyzed customer satisfaction and program performance data and translated survey results into actionable insights.
Action
Using Microsoft Excel, I conducted question-level analysis, monthly summaries, surveyor summaries, nurse monitor review, sentiment analysis, charts, and executive-level reporting.
Result
The analysis identified key trends, service strengths, gaps, and improvement opportunities, supporting more informed decision-making and quality improvement.
Learning
This project strengthened my ability to turn raw data into meaningful insights and communicate findings clearly for healthcare stakeholders.
